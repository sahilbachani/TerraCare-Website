What is TerraCare?

TerraCare is your companion in the fight against environmental pollution. Our mission is to bring people together to clean up our world. Using cutting-edge AI technology to find nearby cleanup events, verify completed tasks, and reward your efforts. By participating in cleanups, you not only help beautify your community but also earn rewards and climb the leaderboard.

"Join TerraCare today and be a part of a growing community dedicated to making a tangible difference in the fight against climate change and environmental degradation."
